The game will begin when opening the code.

By entering the number of fuel, the spacecreaft begins to fall.

Winning Policy: You should let the velocity increase by much faster than 10m/s without burning any fuel, then burning large amount of fuel at each time so that the velocity could be decreased drastically. If you just want to keep the velocity around 10m/s at the beginninng of the game, you will be highly likely to lose this game. Of course, you cannot allow the velocity to increase large enough(over 54m/s), because in that case you will not have the chance to decelerate.
